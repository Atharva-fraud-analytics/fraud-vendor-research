3. Enterprise Fraud Management (EFM)

The EFM platform acts as a unified hub monitoring Internet Banking, Mobile Banking, Cards, ATM, Core Banking, and Branch transactions. It identifies complex patterns that span across these traditionally siloed systems. By analyzing the contextual relationship between events across channels in real-time, it intervenes before funds leave the institution.

4. AML Platform

Integrated seamlessly with the EFM side, the AML suite covers the entire compliance lifecycle: Customer Due Diligence (CDD/KYC), Watch-list Screening, Suspicious Activity Monitoring (SAM), and Regulatory Reporting (SAR filing). It leverages the same underlying cross-channel data lake, significantly reducing false positives compared to legacy batch AML systems.

11. Risk Scoring Methodology

  • ▶
    Real-Time Synthesis: The score recalculates instantly upon every event (login, beneficiary addition, payment).
  • ▶
    Component Scoring: Combines monetary anomalies, non-monetary anomalies (geo-location jumps), and network risk.
  • ▶
    Action Thresholds: Scores map to actions: Allow, Step-up Auth (MFA), Block, or Alert.
